I joined PRSSA in the Fall of 2012 and was elected Junior Board Member.  During that time I worked with the entire executive board on any tasks they needed assistance with.  I was also in charge of flyers for events and updating the club’s bulletin board.

In March of 2013 I was elected as Secretary of the chapter.  I was in charge of keeping track of club attendance and all paperwork for club events. 

The President who was elected in March of 2013 had to step down in September 2013, which left available board positions in the club.  I then ran for and was elected for the position of Vice President, which is the position I currently hold.  As Vice President I took on the task of launching Uptown Consulting, our student-run PR firm.  All club members work on teams for the firm and we currently work with 8 clients but have a long list of prospective clients to take on as we grow.
