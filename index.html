About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio!

My name is Caitlin Kempinski.  I am a junior at Manhattan College in Riverdale, NY.  I am majoring in Communication with a concentration in Public Relations and minor in Marketing.  I plan to use this site to showcase my work that I completed through internships and classes and give future employers a more detailed view of myself. 

I have held several internship positions and am actively involved in my college’s chapter of Public Relations Student Society of America [PRSSA].  As a part of PRSSA, we launched a student-run public relations called Uptown Consulting.
