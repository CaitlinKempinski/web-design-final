Writing Examples

The Cabot Picnic Press Release was written by me to the Northeast Driller and was picked up and featured on the front page of the newspaper.

The Big Dog Daddy Press Release was sent to the Scranton Times and they asked for an interview with Eddie and Patti. The resulting article was featured on the front of the People section with large color photos and a long story on their mission.

I was also given the opportunity to write radio copy for Focus 81, an organization in the Scranton area that aims to make a particularly congested part of Interstate 81 a safer place for motorists.
